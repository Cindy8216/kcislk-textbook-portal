Important Information

領取規範 Distribution

G7–G10 課本由學校統一訂購並於開學首日發放。G11–G12 「英文科」由學校採購；其餘科目採預購制（表單於 5 月公告）。

G7–G10 textbooks are distributed on the first day. G11–G12 English is school-purchased; others are pre-order (available in May).

退換政策 Returns

預購書籍恕不接受退書。其餘教材退換請於開學後兩週內至國際處（IPD Office）辦理，逾期不予受理。

Pre-ordered textbooks are non-returnable. Other returns must be processed at the IPD Office within the first 2 weeks.

教材使用時程 Usage Period

多數教材為「全學年」使用。若屬特定單學期教材，將明確標註「第一學期 (Sem 1)」或「第二學期 (Sem 2)」。

Most textbooks are for the full year unless explicitly labeled as "Semester 1" or "Semester 2".

收費參考 Pricing

目前所列金額為 114 學年度 (SY25-26) 費率；115 學年度 (SY26-27) 正式金額將於 7 月份公告。

Prices listed are for SY25-26; SY26-27 official rates will be announced in July.

詳細明細 More Info

如需查閱詳細書目資訊（作者、版本等），請點擊該書目之「ISBN」連結。

For detailed bibliographic info (editions, authors, etc.), please click the "ISBN" link.

⚠️ 重要聲明 Notice

本校保留根據教學需求調整選書之權利。

The school reserves the right to adjust textbook selections based on instructional requirements.
